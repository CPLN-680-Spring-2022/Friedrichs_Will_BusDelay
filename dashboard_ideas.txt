Basic webpage functionality...

The webpage opens, and there is a map of Philadelphia. 
- You zoom in and out. 
- Visible are all bus cancellations in Philadelphia in one color (semi transparent).
- Visible are all bus early arrivals in philadlphia.in a different color (semi transparent). 

Each point on the map, when hovered on the cursor, display information associated with a given stop.


Additions I. 
- You can check boxes to make certain things appear (cancellations only, early arrivals only, etc.).
- Bus routes are visible - clicking on them brings up a dialogue box with additional information.

- All of the following are functional, and you can flip between days of data.
